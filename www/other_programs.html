  2. dotvim.tar.bz2 - .vim directory tarball. Untar in your home directory as .vim.
  3. zshrc - ZSH config file. Save it in your home directory as .zshrc. If you are stuck with bash and cannot change your shell to zsh, use following in your .bashrc.
    exec zsh --login
