Teaching material

I own Yamaha G-228. I am basically a novice student of classical music and I depend on online tutorials for guidance.

Here are some links for that I found useful:

  1. Classical Guitar: Positioning — start with this one. Very important! Almost all classical guitar players use this positioning. Once comfortable with the positioning, look for other lessons by the Lutemann on Youtube.
  2. Justin Guitar — If you don't want to be limited to classical music, this is the site for you. Blues, Rock and Jazz...
  3. Pawan Jalan’s channel on Youtube — For those who want to play Hindi bollywood songs.
