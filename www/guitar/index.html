I have a classical acoustic guitar (Yamaha G-228 Classical). It sounds perfect to me :)

Anyway... I am a novice student, and I depend on online tutorials for guidance. Listed are some materials that I found quite helpful.

Teaching material

  1. Classical Guitar: Positioning - Start with this. Almost all classical guitar players use this positioning.
    Once comfortable with the positioning, look for other lessons by the same guy (Lutemann) on Youtube.
  2. Justin Guitar - If you don't wnat to be limited to classical music, this is the site for you. Blues, Rock and Jazz.
  3. Pawan Jalan's channel on Youtube - For those who want to play Hindi bollywood songs.
